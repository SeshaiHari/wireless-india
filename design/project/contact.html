FAQ

Asked
often.

Quick answers to the things we hear at the counter every week. Don't see yours? Just WhatsApp us.

Can I listen / try things before buying?

Yes. We have a demo bar for headphones, a small listening room for soundbars and home theatre, and most peripherals are out of the box for you to try. Just walk in.

Do you give GST invoices?

Always. Just share your company name and GSTIN at the counter, or in the enquiry form above for bulk orders.

Do you deliver outside the city?

Yes — anywhere in India via courier. In-city is usually same-day or next-day. Outside, 2–5 days depending on location.

What about returns & warranty?

Brand warranty on everything. For DOA (dead on arrival), bring it back within 7 days for a no-questions replacement. For other issues, we'll help coordinate with the brand service centre.

Do you offer repairs?

Cable termination, soldering, basic audio repairs — yes. For brand-warranty service we coordinate with the official centre on your behalf.

Can I order online today?

The full online store is on its way — for now, browse here and ping us on WhatsApp with the item; we'll confirm stock and arrange pickup or delivery.
