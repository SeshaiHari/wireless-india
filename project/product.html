About this multimeter.

An auto-ranging pocket digital multimeter — for the curious tinkerer, the home electrician, and the repair counter that just needs a beep and a number.

The AstroAI AM33D is a 4000-count auto-ranging digital multimeter that earns its place in a small toolbox. It reads voltage, current, resistance and continuity quickly and without the guesswork — flip to ohms, and it picks the range for you. Backlit, with a low-battery indicator and a sturdy holster, it survives a drop off a workbench.

We stock it because it's the meter we'd hand a friend who's just getting started, or a regular customer who needs a reliable second meter for the field bag. Bring it back any time for a battery swap or a probe re-tip.

  • Reads AC/DC voltage up to 600V — wall outlets, batteries, USB.
  • 10A current range with separate jack — safe for most workshop circuits.
  • Continuity beep is loud enough to hear over a ceiling fan.
  • Diode & transistor (hFE) test for repair work.
  • Comes with silicone test leads, 9V battery and a paper quick-reference card.
  • Two-year service life on the 9V if used reasonably — auto-off after 15 min.
